OVERVIEW

The world today is changing at an unbelievable pace and skilled engineers are at the forefront of this constant evolution. We at the Faculty of Engineering are well acquainted with this fact and believe evolution is the way forward. With us, students will get access to engineering courses that have been formulated keeping in mind the current on-goings of the engineering arena.

In this advanced technological world, skilled engineers have become the need of the hour. The Faculty of Engineering was formed with a goal to find solutions to the greatest technological and social problems of the 21st century. The students here are trained to become future leaders who will have the capability to lead giant organizations in the emerging corporate landscape. MITSOE is dedicated to providing students with real world and practical academic exposure that will transform them into successful professionals.

DEAN’S MESSAGE

Prof. Dr. Kunjir

Engineering disciplines integrate scientific principles with practically oriented research, providing systems and processes that themselves create ways of acquiring and applying new knowledge. This integration makes engineers a critical thinker for a successful industrial innovation and societal benefits. MIT SoE programmes retain the rigor of engineering while broadening the curriculum to address communication across cultures, management within and across organizations, intellectual property and technology transfer issues, financing innovation, knowledge of regulatory environments, and so on.

PCIMCA offers “Design Enable Inno-preneurship”, approach to the engineering programme, as design thinking is systematic way to innovation that can produce new products, services and business models for the societal benefits. As a best practice, we offer a practical education using Project Based Learning (PBL) as an educational strategy to teach skills essential to innovation and entrepreneurship. Our programmes also provides integration of cutting-edge engineering knowledge, basic science with humanities and overall holistic development. We offer engineering enrichment programmes in core and specialised areas of Computer science and Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Communication, Mechanical, Civil and Aerospace Engineering.

Strength of our programmes are:

  • Specialized Curriculum to meet Industry requirement.
  • Mandatory Internship program.
  • Entrepreneurship Development.
  • Value Added Programs (VAP).
  • Interdisciplinary Research Studies.
  • State of the Art-Infrastructure.

Our teaching faculty focus on delivering latest knowledge, high quality teaching through innovative learning models. We have among our teaching faculty members, certified trainer for APPLE Swift Programming, CISCO and Juniper networking, AWS Cloud practitioner, Robotic Process Automation, Electric Vehicle etc, experts who are consultants and hold patents and research publications at national and international platforms. With an emphasis on good teaching and an outstanding student experience, we will continue to produce globally competitive graduates and maintain the MIT legacy as the Best Institute for teaching and learning.

MIT SoE students enjoy a cordial and open learning environment that allows them to access high end laboratory, state-of-the-art Library, participate in various extra-curricular and co-curricular activities, and become a members of various clubs at institute and University level like Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Film Club, Technology club etc.

You are here on a brief journey of engineering education that might become the most important years in your life. Years that might change you - if you let it happen and take charge of your own development and transformation. Your own contribution is actually one of four basic components in your education. Your inquisitiveness, your research, your creativity, your review on society requirement.
