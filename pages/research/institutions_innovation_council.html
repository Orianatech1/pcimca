Institution's Innovation Council at a Glance

IIC systematically fosters the culture of Innovation, to encourage, inspire and nurture young students of MIT ADT University by supporting them to work with new ideas and transform them into prototypes. IIC MIT ADTU works inline with the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell (MIC)'s objectives.

During the last three consecutive years IIC MITADT University has achieved:

4 Star rating (highest rating)
in 2021-22
4 Star rating (highest rating)
in 2020-21
5 Star rating (highest rating)
in 2019-20
  • Due to consecutively achieving the highest rating Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell (MIC) has appreciated our performance. (Appreciation Letter by Dipan Sahu).
  • IIC also plays a major role in ARIIA ranking, we have performed in "Excellent Band Category" which is top 50 in private universities in India.
